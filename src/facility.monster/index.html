Facility management, just less monstrous.

Facility monster is a small, fast and scalable facility management platform, perfect for managing customers, bookings and sending invoices.

Huh, Facility Monster?

Why did we build this? Well, we were tired with the large, old and expensive solutions that where out there. We belive that facility managment should be both fun and easy!

What can it do?

  • Invoice tenants
  • Managing bookings
  • Usage monitoring
  • Automatic Invoicing
  • Manage Subscriptions

... and much, much more!

Who is it for?

  • Co-workingspaces
  • The property manager
  • Those with excess inventory
  • Self-storage businesses
  • The property owner

Not sure if its for you? Holla at us!

Powerful Features

Don't disregard Facility Monster because it's cute; it has quite the claws. Let's take a look at some of it's features:

Dead simple bookings

Allow your users to quickly book rooms using your phone or the meeting room screen. All this while automatically handling their billing in the background.

Butter smooth invoicing

Tired of manually creating and administrating invoices? Facility Monster takes care of all that by integrating with Billogram to generate and send invoices.

Trivial customer and user management

It doesn't matter if your customers are large, international institutions or a mom-and-pop shop, Facility Monster is flexible enough to cater to all.
